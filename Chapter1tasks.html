Your First Quest

Before leaving the village you must complete the following tasks. When these things are completed you can move on to the next phase of your quest.

  • Labor to correct some of the damage. Complete three tasks to clean up the house. The tasks must be approved by your mother.
  • Settle affairs in the village. Do a service project for someone outside your family. Minimum of 30 minutes.
  • Take a picture of you with you weapon in a forest background.
  • Give your weapon a name.
  • Write a backstory for your life in the village up to this point in the story. Mimimum of three pages. Revise the story until it is perfect.
  • Publish your backstory and picture online and link to this site.
  • Put together a 72 hour kit in a backpack.
  • Camp outside for one night using only the gear from your 72 hour kit.
