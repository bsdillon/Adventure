The Adventure Begins!

Once this kingdom was a peaceful place,

but when the good king aged

he chose one of his two sons

to inherit the throne.


The younger son was bitter

locking himself away in the Dark Tower.

There he worked his magyks

to bring about the downfall of the kingdom.


He opened a portal to another realm

where he could rule all.

The kingdom was transported to this place

full of thorns and thistles...

...And always, always the Dark Tower.


Now men fight each other face to face,

and only the strong survive.

Nestled in the shadow of the great mountains

Your village has remained unnoticed.



Until now....
