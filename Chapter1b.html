The True and False Tokens

Long ago the king sent men into every corner of the realm to find the symbols of righteousness. The men found more than he intended.

Along with the eleven true tokens of righteousness they found eleven false tokens. Each of the true tokens has a power to fight evil, but the false tokens will fail in that moment.

I was one of those men. When the portal in the Tower was opened, the tokens were scattered. All eleven are scattered upon the face of the land--as are the false tokens. I cannot tell you which are which. I only know that I have found the first...

The old man reaches into his cloak and pulls out his hand to reveal a shimmering trinket. He kneels to open an iron-clad box remains in the ashes of his home. Inside are a pair of weapons. He silently signals each of you to pick up a different weapon and he hangs the trinket from your hilt.

It's name is Faith. To defeat the Prince, you must find the true tokens. You must make your way to the Dark Tower. You must confront his evil with the only power that can stop him. Take up your weapons now and prepare for your journey.
